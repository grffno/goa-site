Griffin Ofiesh (b. 1988) is a registered architect in New York. His work engages architecture across building, interior, and installation scales. He holds a Master of Architecture from Princeton University and a Bachelor of Architecture from Virginia Tech.

Prior to founding GOA, Griffin worked on cultural, institutional, and residential projects at several award-winning firms in NYC. His experience includes the technical design of Lasting Joy Brewery at Auver Architecture; the exterior envelope of the Wellesley College Science Center at SOM; residential developments in New York and London at Thomas Juul-Hansen LLC, including Belgravia Gate; and Legible Pompeii, an installation designed at MOS Architects for the 2014 Venice Architecture Biennale.
